These tires weren't bad for the price but they did have their shortcomings. One of the big issues was these are called all seasons, but they were utterly useless in snow. They're basically hockey pucks when it's cold out and I spun instantly even with an utter lack of torque, just trying to move the car around the yard. They're a decently grippy tire and I only managed to overdrive the tires grip once when I took a corner a little to hot. Road noise is present but far from over powering. They do make a bit of noise on chip seal roads though. Another big issue is if you drive spiritedly in the corners, you will basically scrub the tread off in a real hurry. I'd hate to see these tires after a track day. I changed these tires out at 30,000 km and they were all down to the wear bars. They were also rotated every 5,000 kms which didn't seem to help much apparently.

Size: 205/45R16

Vehicle: 1992 Acura Integra 3 Dr Hatchback GS with 30000 KM of Tire Wear
